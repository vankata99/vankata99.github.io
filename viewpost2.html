Innovations you’ll be using next year

Seamless Sales

Until recently, most mobile devices and PCs have operated on separate platforms. As a result, it was difficult to share and edit files across different devices. In response to this issue, tech companies have begun focusing on providing a more seamless experience across a user’s entire device ecosystem. Windows 10’s Continuum2 can essentially turn your Windows-enabled phone into a PC-and is probably the most far-reaching attempt to date to deliver a seamless experience across devices. Designed to facilitate the switch between traditional and touchscreen modes, Continuum throws out the fixed OS model for a more responsive system that’s ideal for traditional PCs, touchscreen devices, smartphones, and hybrid devices. As device manufacturers and software programmers familiarise themselves with Windows 10 Continuum (and soon Continuum for phones), and experiment with its powerful features, we should expect to see more combined functionality, more innovation, and a stronger focus on seamless experiences.
